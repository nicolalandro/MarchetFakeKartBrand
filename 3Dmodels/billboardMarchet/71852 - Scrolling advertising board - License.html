Blend information

  • Name: Scrolling advertising board
  • Author: mik1190.
  • Premalink: http://www.blendswap.com/blends/view/71852.
  • For Blender 2.52 and up.
  • Description:

    Simple advertising board.

    You can move("scroll") the image inside by rotating the circle surrounding the board: 120 degrees each image for a total of 3 images; it's made using a simple driver and can be useful for animations.

    The internal"panel" is in layer 2, you may need to change the image display ed.

    To move the whole thing just select the base.

    To decrease po ly count change the settings of the bevel modifier.

    A easier method to m ove the image inside was to animate the uv coordinates using the mapping node.
